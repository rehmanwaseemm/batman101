14 February night

The Night We Became Us

On 14 February, I picked you up from IBEX 3, not knowing that an ordinary drive would become one of my favorite memories. We drove around, talked, laughed, and let the night take us wherever it wanted. I had watched Attack on Titan because you recommended it, and when you asked me about Eren's eyes, I lost the bet. But somehow, even losing felt sweet because it was with you. You wanted lily of the valley flowers, so we went all the way to Valencia Town looking for them. We could not find them, but the search itself became part of the memory. We wandered without a perfect plan, you drove the car a little, and later I gave you a sunflower. It was not perfect in the way movies are perfect. It was better. It was real. It was us. A night of small moments that still feel beautiful when I think about them.
